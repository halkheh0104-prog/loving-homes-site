Facilities and services

Premium spaces designed around wellbeing

Every part of Loving Homes has been planned to support comfort, safety, exercise and consistent owner reassurance.

✂️

Grooming salon and spa

Professional grooming and cleaning services help dogs stay healthy, fresh and comfortable during their stay.

🌡️

Heated and cooled rooms

Private rooms with underfloor heating and cooling create a more controlled and relaxing environment.

🚐

Pick-up and drop-off

A transport service improves convenience for owners and supports smooth arrival and departure arrangements.

🌳

Secure outdoor paddocks

Dogs enjoy supervised outdoor movement in safe, enclosed areas built for controlled exercise and play.

🏠

Indoor play barn

Indoor activity space supports play and exercise regardless of weather conditions or seasonal changes.

🩺

24-hour veterinary support

Access to veterinary care adds reassurance and strengthens the hotel’s safety offering for owners.

📷

Pet monitoring updates

Daily photo and progress updates keep owners informed and support trust in the service.

🥏

Forest walks and activities

Structured activities promote enrichment, exercise and mental stimulation throughout the stay.

🌿

Private grass space

Premium dogs can enjoy quieter private outdoor time in a dedicated grass area for comfort and calm.
