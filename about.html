About the hotel

A caring and modern dog hotel built around trust

Loving Homes was developed to give dog owners peace of mind through premium accommodation, attentive staff and safe activities tailored to each pet.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, clean and reassuring environment where dogs receive professional care, enjoyable activity and genuine comfort while owners stay informed and confident.

Our Vision

To become one of Hong Kong’s most trusted dog hospitality brands by combining premium facilities, accessible communication and personalised care.

Why we are different

Built for owners who expect more than basic boarding

🛏️

Comfort

Private rooms with heating and cooling help dogs rest in a calm and controlled environment.

🩺

Safety

Veterinary support is available 24 hours a day for reassurance and fast response if needed.

📷

Transparency

Owners receive updates and photos so they can stay connected to their dog’s daily experience.

🌳

Enrichment

Daily walks, outdoor space, indoor play and personalised routines keep dogs active and engaged.
